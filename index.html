About

Dr. B. Sathiyabhama, the Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Sona College of Technology, has over 29 years of teaching experience. She holds a Ph.D. from NIT Tiruchirappalli and specializes in Data Mining, Big Data, and Health Informatics. Dr. Sathiyabhama has received the Best Faculty Award for her contributions to academics. Her notable paper, "Big Data in Healthcare Systems" is widely recognized for its innovative approach. A proud member of IEEE, ACM, and other professional organizations, she is dedicated to advancing education, mentoring students and solving complex challenges in her field.
